Transfer

A Transfer represents the transfer of an amount of currency from source to target.

A Transfer is held in a Wallet (holding the amount's currency); the Transfer requires a fee to complete. Once the transfer is signed/submitted it can be identified by a TransferHash. Once the transfer has been included in the currency's blockchain it will have a TransferConfirmation.

A Transfer is Equatable but not Hashable; Hashable would naturally be implemented in terms of the TransferHash however that hash isn't available until after a transfer is signed.
