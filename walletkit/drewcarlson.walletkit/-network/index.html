A Blockchain Network.

Networks are created based from a cross-product of block chain and network type. Specifically {BTC, BCH, ETH, ...} x {Mainnet, Testnet, ...}. Thus there will be networks of BTC-Mainnet, BTC-Testnet, ..., ETH-Mainnet, ETH-Testnet, ETH-Rinkeby, ...
